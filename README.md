## About

I'm an AI/ML engineer working mostly in computer vision, NLP, and applied deep learning — the kind of models that end up making a decision, not just a prediction. That means I spend as much time on the data side (annotation, ETL, pipeline design) as I do on the model itself, because a good model on bad data is worthless.

Most of my work lands in a few domains: agriculture, satellite imagery, and increasingly, space systems and on-orbit inference. I like problems where the output has to actually work for someone — a farmer checking a crop, a mission planner reading telemetry — not just look good in a notebook.

<br>

## What I work on

**Applied AI/ML**
Crop disease detection from imagery, satellite-based land use and change detection, forecasting models for markets and logistics, and transformer-based NLP for local-language interfaces.

**Data engineering & annotation**
Real-time ETL from IoT devices, satellites, and sensors. Annotation workflows for image, text, and video data. Data versioning and lineage, and tuning infrastructure for training and inference workloads.

**Full-stack AI systems**
Python backends (Django, FastAPI, Flask) serving models through REST and GraphQL, React/Next.js frontends, and Docker-based CI/CD so the whole thing is reproducible, not just running on my laptop.

**Space systems**
Edge inference for satellites, ground station processing for telemetry and imagery, and climate/agriculture monitoring from orbit. This is the area I'm most excited to grow into.

## Featured projects

**AgriTech Harvest Hub** — *live*
A platform helping smallholder farmers with AI-driven insights: crop disease detection (95%+ accuracy), real-time market price forecasting, a multilingual chatbot, and IoT + satellite soil moisture data fused into one view.

**CosmicIntel** — *research prototype*
Satellite imagery analysis for land use and disaster response, using multi-spectral segmentation (U-Net) and change detection on Sentinel-2 and Landsat data, with inference optimized to run on edge GPUs.
